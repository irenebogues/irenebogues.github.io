Irene Bogues

Irene understands that today’s Executive Assistants and Office Managers need more than administrative, organizational and communication skills–they will be called upon to transform business culture through leading-edge technology. In her current role as Executive Assistant to the Head of Digital and VP of Digital Group at Infosys, she has proven that she can exceed the high expectations of a global departmental head, interact effectively with technology VPs, tackle special projects, conduct valuable research and simultaneously oversee all day-to-day administrative operations.

In Irene’s previous role as Office Manager and Executive Assistant to the CEO of Gotham Life Coach, Irene transformed an underperforming workplace into an efficient, technology-savvy organization by leveraging her highly effective suite of organizational and computer skills. Irene Bogues overcomes workplace disorganization wherever possible, using her administrative and technical skill sets to create streamlined workflows, implement clear reporting and operations procedures and eliminate unnecessary or redundant inefficiencies.

In her free time, Irene is a chef, artist, technology enthusiast and dog lover. You might find Irene with a camera in hand or summoning the culinary gods to intervene on her behalf. Her perfect world dream hobby involves developing nonprofit coding camps for at-risk women and children in the US and beyond... bringing bleeding-edge employment opportunities to the world’s most disenfranchised and disadvantaged.
